Canada's Premier Casino Resorts in 2026

    
      

Across Canada, casino resorts combine upscale accommodation with engaging entertainment, drawing visitors who want relaxation and recreation in a single destination. These properties typically showcase expansive gaming areas equipped with modern slot machines and well-known table games such as blackjack, roulette, and poker. Many of these resorts are developed to balance leisure and convenience. Fallsview Casino Resort – Niagara Falls, Ontario

    

Gaming

    

Fallsview Casino Resort features one of Canada's largest gaming spaces, offering a substantial selection of slot machines and table games. Guests can enjoy traditional classics alongside newer game variations within a vibrant and well-managed environment.

    

Accommodation & Amenities

    

The resort provides stylish guest rooms and expansive suites, many offering views of Niagara Falls. Amenities include a full-service spa, fitness centre, and heated indoor pool.

    

Dining

    

A diverse collection of restaurants caters to a wide range of preferences, from upscale dining experiences to relaxed, casual options.

    

Location

    

Situated steps from Niagara Falls, the property blends high-energy entertainment with scenic natural surroundings.

  
  
    

River Rock Casino Resort – Richmond, British Columbia

    

Gaming

    

River Rock Casino Resort delivers a broad gaming selection, including slot machines, electronic gaming terminals, and popular table games such as blackjack and poker. The layout is spacious and accessible for guests.

    

Accommodation & Amenities

    

Rooms and suites feature contemporary décor focused on comfort and practicality. Guests also have access to a spa, indoor pool, fitness facilities, and convenient connections to Richmond and downtown Vancouver.

    

Dining

    

On-site restaurants offer a mix of international dishes and regional cuisine, ranging from fine dining to casual fare.

    

Location

    

Overlooking the Fraser River, the resort combines waterfront scenery with a relaxed yet energetic setting.

  
  
    

Casino de Montréal – Montréal, Quebec

    

Gaming

    

As one of the largest casinos in the country, Casino de Montréal provides an extensive array of slot machines and table games designed to accommodate a variety of gaming preferences.

    

Accommodation & Amenities

    

Although the casino does not include an attached hotel, visitors can choose from numerous nearby accommodations in downtown Montréal, making it easy to combine entertainment with city exploration.

    

Dining

    

Several restaurants and lounges are located within the venue, many inspired by Québec's distinctive culinary heritage.

    

Location

    

Positioned on Île Notre-Dame, the casino enjoys a unique waterfront setting that reflects Montréal's dynamic cultural character.

  
  
    

Casino Rama Resort – Orillia, Ontario
